# UGC Advertisement Prompt

## Objective

Generate a high-converting User Generated Content (UGC) advertisement for a reusable stainless steel water bottle.

## Role

Act as an experienced digital marketing strategist and UGC creator.

## Product

EcoGlow Stainless Steel Water Bottle

## Target Audience

* College Students
* Office Professionals
* Gym Enthusiasts
* Travelers
* Eco-conscious Consumers

## Requirements

Create a natural and engaging 30-second advertisement.

Include:

* Attention-grabbing hook
* Personal storytelling
* Product demonstration
* Key benefits
* Emotional connection
* Strong Call-to-Action

## Product Features

* BPA Free
* Keeps drinks cold for 24 hours
* Keeps drinks hot for 12 hours
* Leak-proof design
* Eco-friendly
* Lightweight
* Stylish design

## Tone

* Friendly
* Authentic
* Conversational
* Trustworthy

## Output Format

### Hook

### Introduction

### Personal Experience

### Benefits

### Call-to-Action

## Expected Outcome

Generate a realistic UGC advertisement that feels like it was created by a satisfied customer rather than a traditional brand advertisement.
