# Scroll-Stopping Hooks Prompt

## Objective

Generate engaging hooks for short-form social media advertisements.

## Prompt

Act as a professional social media copywriter.

Generate 20 unique hooks for a reusable water bottle.

Requirements:

* Maximum 12 words
* Create curiosity
* Focus on benefits
* Encourage viewers to keep watching
* Suitable for Instagram Reels
* Suitable for Facebook Ads
* Suitable for YouTube Shorts

## Example Style

* I never expected this bottle to change my daily routine!
* This simple habit saved me money every week.
* You're probably drinking water the wrong way.
* My gym bag finally has the perfect bottle.
* This bottle keeps my drinks cold all day.

Generate twenty completely different hooks.
