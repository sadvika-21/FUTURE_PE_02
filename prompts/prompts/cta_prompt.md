# Call-to-Action Prompt

## Objective

Generate persuasive Call-to-Action (CTA) statements for social media advertisements promoting the EcoGlow Stainless Steel Water Bottle.

## Prompt

Act as a professional marketing copywriter.

Create 20 short and compelling CTAs that encourage customers to purchase the EcoGlow Stainless Steel Water Bottle.

### Requirements

* Maximum 10 words each
* Friendly and persuasive tone
* Action-oriented
* Suitable for Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube Shorts

### Examples

* Shop Now
* Order Yours Today
* Stay Hydrated Every Day
* Make the Switch Today
* Join the Eco-Friendly Movement
* Upgrade Your Hydration
* Buy Now and Save
* Grab Yours Before It's Gone
* Stay Cool Anywhere
* Drink Better Every Day

Generate 20 unique CTA statements.
